📚 What is TDEE?

BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) = Calories your body burns at complete rest to maintain organs, breathing, and heartbeat.

TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure) = BMR × Activity multiplier = Your actual daily calorie need.

🟢 Eat 500 kcal below TDEE → Lose ~0.5 kg/week
🔴 Eat 500 kcal above TDEE → Gain ~0.5 kg/week
⚖️ Eat at TDEE → Maintain current weight
⚠️ Never eat below 1200 kcal (women) or 1500 kcal (men) without medical supervision.

🏆 30-Day Indian Weight Loss Plan
Week-by-week authentic Indian meal plan. Starts at 1800 kcal → reduces to 1400 kcal by Week 4. Follow consistently for 3–5 kg loss in 30 days.
🩺 Diabetic Diet Guide for Indians
Manage blood sugar naturally through Indian food. Foods are ranked by Glycemic Index (GI). Low GI = safest for diabetics. Always consult your doctor.
🟢 Low GI (<55) — Best
  • Moong Dal — GI 25
  • Rajma — GI 28
  • Rolled Oats — GI 55
  • Brown Rice — GI 50
  • Chickpeas — GI 28
  • Barley (Jau) — GI 28
  • Sweet Potato (boiled) — GI 44
🟡 Medium GI (55–70) — Moderate
  • Whole Wheat Roti — GI 62
  • Basmati Rice — GI 58
  • Jowar Roti — GI 62
  • Corn (Makka) — GI 65
  • Mango (ripe) — GI 55
  • Banana (unripe) — GI 52
🔴 High GI (>70) — Avoid
  • White Rice — GI 72
  • Maida / White Bread — GI 85
  • Sugary Drinks — GI 97
  • Poha (instant) — GI 76
  • Deep-fried Potato — GI 85
  • Packaged Biscuits — GI 80+
✅ Best Foods for Diabetics
🌿Karela (Bitter Gourd) — natural blood sugar control
🫘Dal (all types) — low GI + high protein
🥬Leafy greens — palak, methi, spinach
🥚Eggs — zero carbs, high protein
🐟Fish — omega-3, anti-inflammatory
🫙Plain curd — low GI, probiotics
🌰Almonds & walnuts — 10–15/day
🧅Onion & garlic — helps insulin sensitivity
🥣Oats — beta-glucan lowers glucose
❌ Foods to Strictly Avoid
🍚White rice — use brown rice instead
🍞Maida products — bread, naan, puri
🥤Cold drinks, packaged fruit juice
🍰Sweets, mithai, cakes, chocolates
🍟Deep fried — samosa, pakora, chips
🍌Overripe banana, mango in excess
🧂High-sodium foods — raises BP
🍿Namkeen, packaged snacks
☕Chai with 3+ teaspoons sugar
🕐 Eat every 3–4 hours — never skip meals. Skipping causes dangerous blood sugar crash then spike.
🚶 Walk 20–30 min after meals — proven to reduce post-meal glucose spike by up to 30%.
💧 8–10 glasses of water daily — dehydration raises blood sugar levels significantly.
🌿 Methi (fenugreek) water — soak 1 tsp seeds overnight, drink in morning. Proven blood sugar benefit.
🥗 Eat vegetables first, then protein, then carbs — reduces glucose spike by up to 40%.
🩺 Check HbA1c every 3 months. Target <7% for well-controlled diabetes. Regular monitoring is key.
